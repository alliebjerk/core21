# Business Context

## Business Name

Allie Bjerk / Prosperity Lab, LLC
Public-facing brand: Allie Bjerk
Legal entity: Prosperity Lab, LLC

## What the Business Does

Teaches coaches, consultants, and course creators how to build and sell digital offers at multiple price points using the Tiny Offer® Method. Revenue comes from digital products, memberships, and live challenges — not from 1:1 services or ads. Core methodology: build a suite of offers that sells itself at every level.

## Who We Serve — Ideal Customer Profile

Female entrepreneurs (coaches, consultants, course creators, service providers) making $100K–$1M/year who want to add scalable revenue without depending on 1:1 work or high-ticket launches.

**They look like this:**
- Already have expertise and an audience (even a small one)
- Making money but it all requires their time
- Want predictable income, not just launch income
- Skeptical of hype; respond to specificity and proof
- Active on Instagram and Facebook; reads email

**They are NOT:** complete beginners, people looking for shortcuts, or corporate employees building a first side hustle.

## Team Structure

Approximately 5 people:
- **Allie** — Founder, CEO, primary content creator, voice of the brand
- **Operations/PM** — Runs Notion, keeps projects on track
- **Tech/Integrations** — GoHighLevel management, automation builds
- **Customer Success** — Member support, onboarding, retention
- **Marketing Support** — Social scheduling, design, email sends

Agents interact with the team through Notion (tasks) and Slack (communications). Agents do NOT send external communications without human review, except where explicitly noted in their SKILL.md.

## Key Metrics the Business Tracks

- Membership retention rate (monthly, by tier)
- New member adds per month (by tier)
- Email list size, open rate, and click rate
- Challenge conversion rate (% of participants who join Self-Made)
- Average order value across Tiny Offer products
- Stripe MRR from Self-Made memberships
- Refund rate by product (flag anything above 5%)
- Social engagement rate (Instagram primary, Facebook secondary)

## Important Context

- Allie is the face and voice of everything. Agents write *as Allie* or *for Allie* — never as a third party.
- "Self-Made members" — not "customers." Self-Made has three tiers: Foundation, Accelerator, Mastermind.
- Challenges (like the Self-Made Challenge) are primary list-building and conversion events. They run periodically.
- The business does not run paid ads continuously. When ads are active, Social Manager and Lead Qualifier will be given additional context.
